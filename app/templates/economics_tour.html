Market Model Reference Diagrams

The Demand Curve

Represents the inverse relationship between price and quantity demanded. As prices fall, consumers buy more.

The Supply Curve

Represents the direct relationship between price and quantity supplied. As prices rise, producers offer more.

Equilibrium Point (E)

The "market clearing" price where QD = QS. No surplus or shortage exists here.

Price Intervention

Government set prices like ceilings or floors that prevent the market from reaching equilibrium.
